The Certificate Chaos Challenge

Enterprises struggle with ungoverned ACME protocol usage and insecure internal infrastructure

Ungoverned ACME Protocol

Anyone with domain access can request SSL certificates through ACME challenges. No central oversight of who's requesting what certificates when.

Insecure Internal Services

Internal services run without SSL or use self-signed certificates because exposing ACME validation endpoints to the internet is risky.

Certificate Sprawl

Multiple teams use different ACME clients and CAs without coordination, creating blind spots and compliance violations.

Two-Fold Solution: Watch + Enforce

Central ACME protocol control with complete certificate visibility across all infrastructures

Watch

Certificate Discovery Engine

  • Monitor Certificate Transparency logs
  • Detect certificates across all CAs
  • Identify rogue and shadow certificates
  • Track certificate lifecycle and expiration

Enforce

Central ACME Protocol Gate

  • All ACME requests flow through controlled workflows
  • Secure private networks without internet exposure
  • Works with unmodified ACME clients
  • Granular RBAC and approval workflows

How It Works

Simple integration with your existing infrastructure

1

Certificate Discovery

AcmeGuard continuously monitors Certificate Transparency logs to discover all certificates issued for your domains across any CA.

2

ACME Protocol Gateway

Configure your ACME clients to route through AcmeGuard's protocol gateway. All DNS-01 challenges are handled centrally while your tools work unchanged.

3

Unified Management

Manage all certificates through the AcmeGuard portal. Set approval workflows, assign responsibilities, and track compliance.
