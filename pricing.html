Pricing FAQ

Why the hybrid pricing model?

We use a base platform fee + scale component because certificate management has fixed infrastructure costs plus variable support needs. This gives you predictable pricing while only scaling costs with actual growth.

Why not charge per certificate?

We think charging per certificate creates the wrong incentives. Security shouldn't be a line item that grows with good practices. Our model encourages you to manage as many certificates as you need.

What happens when I exceed user limits?

Small Teams includes 10 users, Enterprise includes 50 users, then â‚¬15/month per additional user. We designed these limits to be generous enough that you shouldn't need to share accounts - and if you're still sharing accounts beyond these limits, you should probably address that security risk before handling certificates. Your customers and your auditor will be happier that way.

Are SSO and API access really included in all tiers?

Yes! Unlike most enterprise software, we include SSO/OIDC and API access in every tier. Essential features shouldn't be held hostage for higher pricing - they're part of basic security and integration needs.

Any discounts available?

Yes! We offer discounts for early adopters, educational institutions, and multi-year commitments. We also value our early customers and will keep that in mind as we grow.

Can we try before we buy?

Of course. We're happy to set up a pilot program so you can see the value firsthand. No risk, no commitments until you're convinced it works for your environment.

What about custom requirements?

On-premises deployment is available starting with Enterprise tier. Our Custom tier adds custom integrations, and dedicated SLAs. We work with you to build a solution that fits your specific infrastructure and compliance needs.

Will you keep these transparent prices?

Yes! We hate "contact sales" as much as you do. These prices are here to stay - no sales games, no mandatory demos to see pricing. Just clear, predictable costs so you can make informed decisions.

What about open source?

We're considering open-sourcing core components while building a sustainable business around enterprise features and hosting. Let us know what matters most to you.
