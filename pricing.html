Pricing FAQ

Why don't you have fixed prices yet?

We'd rather build pricing with our customers than impose a model that doesn't fit real-world usage. Enterprise certificate management varies wildly between organizations.

Will you charge per certificate?

No. We think charging per certificate creates the wrong incentives. Security shouldn't be a line item that grows with good practices.

What about user-based pricing?

We're considering a hybrid model: a flat rate base with per-user costs only above a certain threshold. This avoids penalizing smaller teams while acknowledging that support requirements scale with organization size. Pure per-user pricing from user #1 creates wrong incentives - certificate management involves many stakeholders, and we don't want pricing that discourages good security practices.

Any discounts for early customers?

Absolutely. Early customers get preferential pricing and grandfathered rates as we evolve. You're helping us build the product - we want to make it worth your while.

Can we try before we buy?

Of course. We're happy to set up a pilot program so you can see the value firsthand. No risk, no commitments until you're convinced.

Will you keep the "Contact sales" model when pricing is defined?

No, absolutely not. We also buy software and know how frustrating it is to jump through sales hoops just to see a price. Once we finalize our pricing model, you'll see transparent pricing on this page. No mandatory demos, no sales games. Just clear pricing so you can make informed decisions.
