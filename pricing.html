FAQ

Common pricing questions

Why the hybrid model?

Certificate management has fixed infrastructure costs plus variable support needs. A base platform fee plus a scale component reflects that. Predictable upfront, costs grow only when your usage actually does.

Why not charge per certificate?

Per-certificate fees create the wrong incentives, security shouldn't get more expensive when you do more of it. Our model says: manage as many certificates as you need.

What happens when I exceed the user limit?

Small teams includes 10 seats, Enterprise includes 50. Above that, €15 / month per extra user. The limits are generous enough that you shouldn't need to share accounts, and if you are, your auditor would rather you didn't.

SSO and API access, really in every tier?

Yes. SSO / OIDC and API access are baseline security and integration needs, not features to hold hostage behind a higher SKU.

Are there discounts?

Yes, early adopters, educational institutions, and multi-year commitments. We also remember our first customers as we grow.

Can we try before we buy?

Of course. Pilot programmes are on the table. No commitment until you've seen it work in your environment.

What about custom requirements?

On-premises deployment starts at Enterprise. The Custom tier adds bespoke integrations and contractual SLAs. We'll shape it to your infrastructure and compliance posture.

Will these prices stay public?

Yes. We hate "contact sales for pricing" as much as you do. The numbers above are here to stay, no sales games to see them.

Open source?

We're considering open-sourcing core components while building a sustainable business around the enterprise tier and hosting. Tell us what would matter most to you.
